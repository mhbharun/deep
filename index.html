Who We Are ?

Deep2 Neuron is an initiative towards learning new technology. It is a platform which helps students to turn their ideas into an innovation. Using this platform students can explore various new things in the field of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science and lot more. Through this platform students not just learn new things, but they will also be allowed to practically experiment the new things that they have learned.
